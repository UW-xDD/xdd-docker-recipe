Equation (5), involving rates, has two disadvantages: (1) rates are a derived measure and, hence, can be less desirable than the direct measure of ZJE and (2) the number of X, Y pairs available for regression of rates and remaining time is always one less than the number of originally selected Z/E, t points on upper or lower bounds.
